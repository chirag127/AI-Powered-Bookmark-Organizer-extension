About the Extension

The AI-Powered Bookmark Organizer is a browser extension designed to help users manage their bookmarks efficiently. It uses artificial intelligence to automatically categorize and tag bookmarks, suggest related content, and archive unused ones, ensuring a tidy and organized collection.

This tool is ideal for researchers, students, content creators, and professionals who save many web pages and struggle with manual organization. Our AI-powered solution saves you time and keeps your digital resources accessible and well-structured.

Key Features

Automatic Categorization & Tagging

The extension analyzes bookmark content using AI to assign relevant categories and tags. For example, a tech article might be tagged "Technology" and "AI" automatically.

Content Suggestion

Based on your existing bookmarks, the extension suggests related content that may be of interest, enhancing discovery and expanding your resources.

Archiving Old Bookmarks

The extension identifies bookmarks that haven't been accessed for a user-defined period and moves them to an archive, helping declutter your bookmark bar.

Search & Filter

Search for bookmarks using keywords, categories, or tags, making it easy to locate specific saved pages with powerful filtering options.

Customization

Create custom categories, tags, and archiving rules to personalize your bookmark organization according to your preferences.

Installation Instructions

1

Download the Extension

Clone or download the extension from the GitHub repository.

2

Open Extensions Page

In Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions/. In Firefox, navigate to about:addons and select "Extensions". In Edge, navigate to edge://extensions/.

3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle on "Developer mode" in the top-right corner (Chrome/Edge) or click the gear icon and select "Debug Add-ons" (Firefox).

4

Load Unpacked Extension

Click "Load unpacked" (Chrome/Edge) or "Load Temporary Add-on" (Firefox) and select the extension folder from the downloaded repository.

5

Configure the Extension

Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar and follow the setup instructions to configure your preferences.

How to Use

Using the AI-Powered Bookmark Organizer is simple and intuitive:

  1. Add Bookmarks: Save web pages as you normally would using your browser's bookmark feature.
  2. Automatic Organization: The extension will automatically categorize and tag your bookmarks based on their content.
  3. View Organized Bookmarks: Click on the extension icon to view your organized bookmarks, sorted by categories and tags.
  4. Search and Filter: Use the search bar and filters to find specific bookmarks quickly.
  5. Customize: Adjust categories, tags, and archiving rules in the extension settings to suit your preferences.
  6. Discover Related Content: Explore suggested content related to your bookmarks to discover new resources.
