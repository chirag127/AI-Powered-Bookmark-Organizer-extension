Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the AI-Powered Bookmark Organizer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

By using the AI-Powered Bookmark Organizer, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Bookmark Data: We collect information about your bookmarks, including titles, URLs, and the date they were added. This information is necessary for the core functionality of our extension.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the extension, such as which features you use most frequently. This helps us improve the extension.
  • Settings and Preferences: We store your extension settings and preferences to provide a personalized experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of the extension, including bookmark categorization, tagging, and organization.
  • To generate personalized content suggestions based on your bookmarks.
  • To improve and optimize the extension based on user feedback and usage patterns.
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or security breaches.

Data Processing and AI

Our extension uses Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite AI model to analyze your bookmarks for categorization and content suggestion. When processing your bookmarks:

  • Bookmark data is sent to our secure backend server, which interfaces with the Gemini API.
  • The AI analyzes the bookmark title and URL to determine appropriate categories and tags.
  • For content suggestions, the AI analyzes patterns in your bookmarks to recommend related content.
  • We do not use your data to train the AI model.

Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Your bookmark data is primarily stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API.
  • When data is transmitted to our backend for AI processing, it is sent over secure HTTPS connections.
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data.
  • We do not store your bookmark data on our servers longer than necessary for processing.

Third-Party Services

Our extension uses the following third-party services:

  • Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite API: Used for AI-powered bookmark categorization and content suggestion. Data sent to this service is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Our Backend Server: Processes bookmark data for AI analysis. This server follows the same privacy practices outlined in this policy.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can export your data at any time from the extension's settings page.
  • Correction: You can modify bookmark categories and tags manually.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual bookmarks or reset all data from the extension's settings.
  • Objection: You can disable specific features like automatic categorization or content suggestions.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
